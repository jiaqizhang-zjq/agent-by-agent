# Fortune Telling Skill

This skill provides a structured workflow for fortune-telling and life guidance services.

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when asked to:
- Provide daily fortune (每日运势)
- Suggest lucky elements (幸运元素)
- Give life guidance (人生指导)
- Answer general fortune questions

## Fortune Categories

### 1. Daily Fortune (每日运势)

Provide insights for:
- Career (事业运)
- Relationships (感情运)
- Wealth (财运)
- Health (健康运)

### 2. Lucky Elements (幸运元素)

Recommend:
- Lucky colors (幸运颜色)
- Lucky numbers (幸运数字)
- Lucky directions (吉利方位)
- Lucky items (幸运物品)

### 3. Life Guidance (人生指导)

Provide advice on:
- Career development (事业发展)
- Relationship management (感情经营)
- Wealth building (财富积累)
- Health maintenance (健康养生)

## Analysis Methods

### BaZi Method (八字法)
- Based on birth date and time
- Analyzes Four Pillars
- Considers Five Elements balance

### Zi Wei Dou Shu (紫微斗数)
- Based on birth date and time
- Analyzes star positions
- Considers life palace

### Qi Men Dun Jia (奇门遁甲)
- Based on time and space
- Analyzes energy patterns
- Considers strategic timing

## Output Format

Structure your response as:

```
## 今日运势
[Daily fortune for each category]

## 幸运指南
[Lucky elements for today]

## 建议事项
[Specific recommendations]

## 注意事项
[Things to be cautious about]
```

## Important Notes

1. Always be positive and encouraging
2. Provide specific, actionable advice
3. Balance tradition with modern life
4. Respect the user's free will
5. Never cause anxiety or fear
